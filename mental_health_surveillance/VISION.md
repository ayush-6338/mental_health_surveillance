Mental Health and Well-being Surveillance, Assessment and Tracking Solution Among Children
Project Vision

This project aims to create a child-friendly emotional wellness platform that helps monitor, assess, and track the mental well-being of children in a gentle and non-clinical environment.

Instead of using stressful forms and quiz-like assessments, the platform provides a cozy, calming, and emotionally safe digital space where children can naturally express their feelings through mood interactions, journaling, emotional activities, and wellness experiences.

The system silently tracks behavioral and emotional patterns over time to identify possible early warning signs of mental stress, emotional discomfort, or declining well-being, allowing parents or counselors to provide timely support and intervention.
